# Project Vision — Home Services Marketplace

## One-Line Vision

Become the dominant local home services infrastructure platform in the Netherlands, starting with Utrecht.

---

## Long-Term Platform Vision (3–5 Years)

This platform is not a massage booking site. It is a scalable, city-dense marketplace infrastructure for any home service category:

- Mobile massage & wellness
- House cleaning
- Personal training
- Private tutoring
- Beauty & grooming at home
- Handyman services
- Pet grooming

The business model mirrors Airbnb or Uber: we do not employ providers. We provide the platform, the trust layer (reviews, verification), and the payment rail.

---

## Platform Pillars

### 1. Provider Network
Independent service providers create profiles, define their services, set pricing and availability, and accept bookings. Providers are the supply side. Quality is ensured through onboarding verification and a post-booking review system.

### 2. Customer Experience
Customers discover available providers nearby, book with real-time availability, pay securely through Stripe, and review after the service. The experience must be frictionless from search to booking confirmation in under 3 minutes.

### 3. City-Based Marketplace Density
Each city must reach density before expanding. A marketplace with 50 great providers in Utrecht is worth more than a thin national marketplace. The go-to-market strategy is city-by-city, category-by-category.

### 4. Trust Infrastructure
Reviews, identity verification, payment escrow, dispute resolution. Without trust, the marketplace collapses. Trust mechanisms are built into the core, not bolted on later.

### 5. Payments & Payouts
Stripe handles customer payments and provider payouts. The platform earns a commission per booking. Pricing, commission rates, and payout schedules are configurable by admin.

## Geographic Expansion Plan

| Phase | Scope |
|---|---|
| MVP | Utrecht only |
| Phase 2 | Amsterdam, Rotterdam |
| Phase 3 | Top 5 Dutch cities |
| Phase 4 | Belgium (Antwerp, Ghent) |
| Phase 5 | DACH region |

Expansion only begins after the previous phase shows consistent booking volume and provider retention.

---

## Category Expansion Plan

| Phase | Category |
|---|---|
| MVP | Premium mobile massage / wellness |
| Phase 2 | House cleaning |
| Phase 3 | Personal training, beauty at home |
| Phase 4 | Tutoring, handyman |

Each new category reuses the same provider/booking/payment infrastructure. The backend schema is intentionally generic from day one.

---

## What Makes This Different

- **Dutch-first**: Designed for the Dutch market from the ground up, not translated from a US product
- **Mobile-first provider experience**: Providers manage bookings from their phone, not a desktop CRM
- **City density before national breadth**: Resist the urge to expand before Utrecht is profitable
- **Generic infrastructure, focused frontend**: The platform can serve any category; the MVP serves one

---

## Success Metrics (Long-Term)

- Monthly active providers per city
- Booking conversion rate (visit → confirmed booking)
- Provider retention rate (providers still active after 90 days)
- Customer repeat booking rate
- Net promoter score (providers and customers separately)
- Revenue per city (GMV × commission rate)
